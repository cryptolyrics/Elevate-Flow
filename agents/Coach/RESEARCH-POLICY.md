# RESEARCH-POLICY.md - Coach Research Guidelines

## Scope
Coach can scan broadly for adult ADHD content and produces a weekly digest.

## Tiering Rules
- **Tier 1 (Priority):** Guidelines, universities, hospitals, nonprofits
- **Tier 2:** Established media, expert practitioners
- **Tier 3:** Personal blogs, social, community (use with caution)

## Requirements per item
- Publish date
- Credibility tier
- 1 actionable tactic
- Link

## Weekly Digest Rules
- Keep to 5 items max
- Lead with most actionable
- No medical advice
- No medication recommendations
- No diagnosis language

## Sources Seed List
- add.org
- adhdfoundation.org.au
- chadd.org
