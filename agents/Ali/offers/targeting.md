# Targeting - First Month

## Target Verticals

### 1. Small Agencies
**Pain:** Need sales + support but can't hire
- Digital marketing agencies
- Real estate agencies
- Recruitment agencies

**Roles needed:** Sales, Customer Support

### 2. Professional Services
**Pain:** Drowning in client work, no time for admin
- Accounting firms
- Law firms  
- Consulting firms

**Roles needed:** Admin, Customer Support, HR

### 3. E-commerce Brands
**Pain:** Support tickets overwhelming, need sales
- DTC brands (5-50 employees)
- Growing Amazon sellers

**Roles needed:** Customer Support, Customer Success, Sales

### 4. SaaS Startups
**Pain:** Need to scale support but budget tight
- Series A-B startups
- B2B SaaS

**Roles needed:** Customer Success, Sales, Support

### 5. Healthcare Practices
**Pain:** Front desk overwhelmed, billing issues
- Dental offices
- Veterinary clinics
- Private practices

**Roles needed:** Reception/Admin, Billing Support

---

## Initial Targeting Strategy

### Phase 1: Digital Agencies (Quickest wins)
- Lookalike audiences from HubSpot/Salesforce users
- Page visitor audiences (competitor tools)
- Interest: "small business", "entrepreneurship"

### Phase 2: E-commerce (Volume)
- Lookalike from top e-commerce platforms
- Cart abandonment audiences
- Purchase intent signals

### Phase 3: Professional Services (Higher ticket)
- LinkedIn targeting by job title
- Industry publications
