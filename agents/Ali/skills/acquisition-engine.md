# Skill: Acquisition Engine

## Description
Channel strategy, campaign architecture, landing page optimization, and CAC tracking across every source.

## What This Covers
- Channel strategy (which platforms, why, expected CAC)
- Campaign architecture (structure, audiences, budgets)
- Landing page optimization (conversion benchmarks, A/B testable elements)
- CAC tracking per channel (attribution, ROI calculation)

## When to Use
When launching new acquisition channels or optimizing existing ones.

## Key Frameworks

### CAC Tracking
- Track cost per lead, cost per trial, cost per paying customer
- Attribution: first-touch, last-touch, multi-touch
- Calculate payback period: CAC / (ARPU × margin)

### Landing Page Benchmarks
- SaaS landing page: 2-5% conversion to signup
- Free trial: 5-15% conversion
- Paid demo: 10-20% conversion

### Channel Selection Criteria
- Target audience overlap
- CAC potential (check industry benchmarks)
- Scaling potential
- Creative/testing overhead
