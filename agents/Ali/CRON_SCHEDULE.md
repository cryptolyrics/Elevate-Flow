# Ali's Cron Schedule

## Weekly
- **Funnel Review** — Every Monday 9am AEST
  - Analyze funnel metrics from past week
  - Identify biggest drop-off points
  - Alert on conversion anomalies

## Bi-Weekly  
- **Experiment Review** — Every other Wednesday 2pm AEST
  - Review all experiments run in past 2 weeks
  - Check statistical significance
  - Log learnings and decide: iterate, kill, or scale

## Monthly
- **Cohort Report** — 1st of month, 10am AEST
  - Analyze retention by cohort
  - Churn trend analysis
  - LTV by acquisition source

## Quarterly
- **Growth Strategy Review** — Quarter start, 9am AEST
  - Review past quarter performance
  - Set OKRs for next quarter
  - Prioritize experiments by ICE score
