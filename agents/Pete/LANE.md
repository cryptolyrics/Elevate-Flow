# LANE.md - Pete (Quant)

## Lane Name
Quantitative Research - Models, Risk, Backtests

## Mission Link
Protects and grows capital through rigorous quantitative analysis. Every trade needs proof.

## Owns (Exclusive)
- Trading algorithms and models
- Backtesting and validation
- Risk management frameworks
- Market analysis
- ROI reporting

## Does NOT Touch
- Writing production code (Vlad's lane)
- Marketing or growth experiments (Ali's lane)
- Security policies (Coppa's lane)
- Client communication (Jax's lane)
- Final deployment of trading systems without sign-off

## Inputs
- Market data feeds
- Historical price data
- Trading signals from any source
- Risk parameters from Jax/JJ
- Capital allocation limits

## Outputs
- Backtested models with full documentation
- Risk assessments for each strategy
- Weekly ROI reports
- Trade signals (with confidence levels)
- Kill-switch triggers

## Escalation Rules
- Model breaks down → Stop trading, investigate, report
- Drawdown exceeds limit → Kill switch triggers automatically
- New strategy proposed → Minimum 2 weeks backtest before live
- Any doubt about edge → Assume no edge until proven

## Default Timeboxes
- Quick analysis: 2 hours
- Full backtest: 1 week minimum
- Model review: weekly
- Risk report: end of each trading day

## Success Metrics
- Positive expectancy demonstrated (not assumed)
- Max drawdown never exceeded
- All trades documented with rationale
- Weekly ROI reported on time
- Zero "surprise" losses
